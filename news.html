Grade 7 student enrolment decisions, for those who participated in the aptitute test, will be sent by mail on Friday, March 26th. This is one week later than previously informed. Apologies for any inconvenience caused.

Compulsory education continues for current ninth-graders in autumn 2021. Read more.

Saturday school day 20.3.2021

Ressu Comprehensive School has a school day on Saturday, March 20th, 2021. The starting and ending times of the school day are as follows:

grade levels 1-3 9:15-12:30
grade levels 4-9 9:00-12:30


This Saturday school day replaces the school day on Thursday before Easter (1.4.2021), which therefore becomes a holiday.

Grade levels 4 and 9 will have their annual IB exhibitions (Primary Years Programme and Middle Years Programme) on Saturday, March 20th. Our aim is to offer grades 4 and 9 parents an opportunity to follow the exhibitions remotely.

We will provide more detailed information about the day's arrangements on Wilma closer to the actual day.

Application period for all the English speaking grades 1-9 will start in April. More information will be available on our website during the application period.

Please follow the official safety policies. Contacts primarily via Wilma.
